What is MonkeyMonie?

MonkeyMonie is a personal finance and investment tracking platform designed to give you a clear, honest picture of your money — without the complexity of traditional finance tools.

Instead of juggling spreadsheets, bank apps, and investment platforms, MonkeyMonie brings everything together into a single, easy-to-understand dashboard.

  • Track spending, balances, and investments in one place
  • Understand where your money is today and where it’s headed
  • Visualize trends with clean, easy-to-read dashboards
  • Make confident decisions without spreadsheets or guesswork

Whether you’re budgeting, investing, or planning long-term goals, MonkeyMonie helps you stay informed and in control of your finances.
